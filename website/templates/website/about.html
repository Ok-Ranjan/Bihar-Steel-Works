About Bihar Steel Works

Tradition. Quality. Innovation.

Who We Are

Founded in Narayanapur, Bihar, Bihar Steel Works is a small-scale yet highly trusted manufacturer of steel furniture products. From steel almirahs to custom-built solutions, we take pride in every weld and every joint.

Our Mission

To bring high-quality, affordable, and durable steel furniture to every home and business in Bihar and beyond.

What Sets Us Apart

  • ✔️ Custom designs to match your needs
  • ✔️ High-quality materials and sustainable production
